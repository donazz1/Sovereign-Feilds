Design Language

Every room adheres to the whole-house design language while maintaining its own distinct character. White shiplap vaulted ceilings with white painted timber beams throughout. Wide plank white oak floors in every room. No arches anywhere — clean rectangular openings throughout.

  • White shiplap vaulted ceilings with white painted timber beams throughout
  • Wide plank white oak floors throughout — no exceptions
  • Black-framed steel grid windows and French doors throughout
  • Brass hardware throughout — fixtures, knobs, pulls, pot fillers
  • Warm putty/greige plaster walls in living and bedroom areas
  • No arches anywhere — clean rectangular openings throughout
  • Each bedroom has its own private garden courtyard access via French doors

The Wellness Circuit

The contrast therapy sequence — sauna → cold plunge → zen terrace → open farmland — is the heart of the wellness wing. The cedar dry sauna seats four with a Harvia or Huum heater. The cold plunge chills to 10–15°C with a natural stone surround. Sliding glass on the full south wall opens the sequence onto the farmland. The outdoor zen terrace has no roof — completely open to sky, with a fieldstone meditation daybed, a shallow still-water basin, and a Japanese maple specimen tree.

  • Full insulated wall separates wellness from utility — no sound, no smell crossover
  • Independent ventilation — negative pressure on utility side
  • Separate exterior entrances for each wing
  • 30 kW solar array on south-facing barn roof — ~75 panels
  • Starlink on barn roof — automatic failover if fiber down

Sovereign Fields

A modern farmhouse bungalow compound on ~50 acres in King Township, Ontario. Designed as a single-story Z-compound, the house faces due south onto open farmland on the Oak Ridges Moraine. The property is being developed as a long-term family retreat for the Azzopardi family.
