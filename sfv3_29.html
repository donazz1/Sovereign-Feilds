The Wellness Circuit

Sauna → cold plunge → zen terrace → open farmland. Cedar dry sauna seats four with Harvia heater. Cold plunge chills to 10–15°C. Sliding glass on the full south wall opens onto the farmland. The outdoor zen terrace is completely open to sky with a fieldstone meditation daybed and shallow water basin.

  • Full insulated wall separates wellness from utility — no sound, no smell crossover
  • Independent ventilation — negative pressure on utility side
  • 30 kW solar array on south-facing barn roof — ~75 panels
  • Starlink on barn roof — automatic failover if fiber down

The Pool & Outdoor Circuit

A geometric saltwater pool positioned on the east side of the house, tucked against the building so the farmland view south remains completely unobstructed. A tanning ledge steps into the shallow end. In-pool LED lighting, automatic cover, and a nearby outdoor shower complete the pool zone. The hot tub sits separately near the veranda fire pit.

  • Geometric pool ~20×30 ft · tanning ledge · saltwater system · automatic cover · in-pool LED lighting
  • Pool positioned east side of house — south farmland view fully preserved
  • Hot tub separate — near veranda fire pit, not attached to pool
  • Pool house east of pool — changing room, equipment storage, outdoor shower
  • Flagstone pool deck throughout · perennial planting beds at edges
  • Full pool automation via Control4/Savant — lighting, temperature, cover

Outdoor Living Zones

  • Covered Veranda — full width of great room south face, tongue-and-groove ceiling, timber posts, integrated lighting
  • Fire Pit Terrace — flagstone, rectangular gas fire pit, hot tub nearby, lounge seating, open farmland views

Sovereign Fields

A modern farmhouse bungalow compound on ~50 acres in King Township, Ontario. Single-story Z-compound facing due south onto open farmland on the Oak Ridges Moraine. Developed as a long-term family retreat for the Azzopardi family.

The House

Single-story bungalow · 3,800–4,000 sq ft · 5 bedrooms with private garden courtyards · Z-compound wing arrangement · Glass-walled great room pavilion

Design Language

White shiplap vaulted ceilings · Wide plank white oak · Black steel grid windows · Brass hardware · No arches — rectangular openings throughout

The Barn

50 × 72 ft · Wellness wing south · Utility wing north · Gym, sauna, cold plunge, zen terrace, workshop, equipment storage · 30 kW solar

Energy

30 kW solar · 3× Tesla Powerwall 3 · Ontario net metering · 400 amp service · 3× EV chargers · Hydronic heated entry court
